Explain photosynthesis in simple terms
Photosynthesis Explained Simply:

Photosynthesis is how plants make their own food using sunlight! Here's the process:

1. Ingredients: Plants take in carbon dioxide from air and water from soil
2. Energy source: Sunlight provides the energy
3. The magic: Chlorophyll (green stuff in leaves) captures sunlight
4. Result: Plants create glucose (sugar) for food and release oxygen
